Good questions

Before you start

How personal is my plan, really?

Very. The questionnaire is just the quick way in. Certified teachers shape your plan from real classes, and when you come in we fine-tune it with you in person, built around your body and your goal.

Do I have to follow the exact plan?

No. Every class stays open to you, the plan is a recommendation, not a cage. You can swap any session, and your teacher helps you fit it to your week.

I've never done Pilates. Will I keep up?

Completely. All levels are welcome, from total beginner to advanced, and every move can be made easier or harder. The teacher teaches from the middle of the room, so you can always follow along. First-timers are the rule here, not the exception.

Is this a real workout, or too easy?

It's low-impact and still demanding. Mostly your own bodyweight and a few props will challenge you, and most members leave the very first class feeling more switched-on and stronger.

What's in the six-week plan?

A curated sequence of classes built around your goal, about twice a week, a teacher you can reach, plus a mat and a welcome gift on your first visit. More than a class pass, a guided path.

Are classes in English or German?

Both. 17 teachers across German and English, take whichever feels more comfortable.

Where are you?

In the heart of Prenzlauer Berg, Berlin. Exact address and directions land in your confirmation.
