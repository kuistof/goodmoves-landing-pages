Quickly answered

Before you grab it

What's included?

Every regular class on the schedule, as often as you like, until 15 September. Same warm room, same teachers, no add-ons to figure out.

How long does it run?

From now until 15 September. That's roughly 10 weeks, about 2.5 months of unlimited movement for one flat price.

Is it really unlimited?

Yes. Book as many classes as you like across the summer. Come twice a week, come five times, it's up to you. We recommend a rhythm, we never lock you to one.

When can I start?

Whenever you like. You pick your start day at checkout, and your Summer Deal runs from there until 15 September. Choose a day this week or further out, it's your call.

What if I can't make a class?

No problem, just cancel in good time. Please note: a late cancellation costs €5 and a no-show costs €14. It keeps our small classes fair for everyone.

Where are you?

In the heart of Prenzlauer Berg, Berlin. Exact address and directions land in your confirmation.
